## Why This Exists

Most GenAI courses are either too expensive ($500–$2,500), too shallow (just prompt engineering), or designed for people who can sit through 4-hour lectures. This roadmap is different:

- **100% free** — every resource is a YouTube video, blog post, paper, or open-source doc
- **ADHD-optimized** — every lesson is 10–30 minutes with a concrete action task and XP reward
- **Project-based** — every week ends with a mini-project and a weekly challenge with a real deliverable
- **2026-accurate** — covers Claude Opus 4.6, GPT-5, Gemini 2.5, OpenClaw, NemoClaw, Paperclip, Hermes Agent, and the full autonomous agent ecosystem
- **30 weeks, 8 phases, 150+ lessons, 35,000 XP** — from zero to fine-tuning custom models and running production AI systems

## 🎯 Learning Philosophy

This curriculum is built on four principles:

**1. Project-Based Learning** — Every week ends with a mini project and a weekly challenge with a real deliverable. You learn by building, not by watching.

**2. ADHD-Optimized** — Every lesson is 10–30 minutes. Every day has one concrete action task. XP rewards create dopamine hits. No 4-hour lectures.

**3. Resource Curation over Content Creation** — We don't rewrite what already exists. We curate the best free resources and add the context, action tasks, and structure that makes them actually stick.

**4. 2026-Accurate** — We track GitHub, Reddit, HackerNews, and research papers weekly. Outdated content gets replaced. The course evolves with the field.
